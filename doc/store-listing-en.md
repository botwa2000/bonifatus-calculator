## About (in-app)

Bonifatus calculates academic rewards from school grades and tracks progress over time.

It exists because rewarding kids for grades fairly is harder than it looks. Most parents who do it — or want to — run into the same questions: how much exactly is a good grade worth compared to an average one? Does a top mark in PE count the same as one in Maths? And if you have two children at different ages, how do you apply the same standard to both without one feeling shortchanged?

The answer is a system everyone can see and trust. You set what each grade is worth, the app keeps the score. Your kids earn against it, you pay out what it says.

No negotiations. No inconsistency. A system the whole family can rely on.
