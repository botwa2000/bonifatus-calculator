• Get a real-time overview of all your children's grades
• Configure bonus factors to reward excellence

• Set up weekly ongoing grade tracking with custom ratios

Supports German grading (1–6) with configurable tier multipliers:
• Tier 1 (grades 1–2): 2.0× bonus
• Tier 2 (grade 3): 1.5× bonus
• Tier 3 (grade 4): 1.0× bonus
